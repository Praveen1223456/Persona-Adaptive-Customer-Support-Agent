INTERFACE LOADING AND BROWSER COOKIE GUIDE

Issue: Dashboard loading animations spinning infinitely or frozen interface panels.

Step 1: Hard Refresh
Force your browser to clear its local network cache by hitting Ctrl + F5 (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac).

Step 2: Clear Storage Assets
Navigate to Browser Settings -> Privacy and Security -> Cookies and Site Data. Select 'Clear data for this domain' and click confirm.

Step 3: Incognito Testing
Launch an isolated Incognito or Private window to check if background browser extensions are interfering with the login scripts.
