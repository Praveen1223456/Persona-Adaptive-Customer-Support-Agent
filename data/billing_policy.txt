CLOUD SERVICES BILLING AND REFUND POLICY

1. Subscription Cycles
All premium plans run on a automated monthly billing sequence initialized on the 1st of every calendar month.

2. Refund Escalations
Our technical agents cannot authorize refunds directly. If a customer experiences duplicate transaction charges, the ticket must immediately be flagged as a billing sensitivity dispute and escalated to a human finance supervisor. 

3. Processing Windows
Approved refunds take 5 to 7 operational banking business days to reflect in the customer's primary account interface ledger.
